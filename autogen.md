# AutoGen — Notes

## Core Idea
The paper introduces a framework where multiple LLM-based agents collaborate through structured conversation to solve complex tasks.

## Key Insight
Instead of relying on a single model, the system assigns different roles (e.g. planner, executor, critic), allowing iterative problem solving.

## Why It Matters
This shifts AI usage from single interactions to structured systems, closer to real workflows and decision processes.

## Personal Interpretation
This approach is particularly relevant for real-world applications, where tasks are rarely isolated and require coordination between multiple steps and roles.

It suggests that the future of AI is not just better models, but better systems built around them.
