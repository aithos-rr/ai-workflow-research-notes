# AutoGen: Enabling Next-Gen LLM Applications via Multi-Agent Conversation

## Link
https://arxiv.org/abs/2308.08155

## Core Idea
The paper introduces a framework where multiple LLM-based agents collaborate through structured conversations to solve complex tasks.

## Key Contribution
Instead of relying on a single model, the system assigns different roles (e.g. planner, executor, critic), enabling iterative and collaborative problem solving.

## Why It Matters
This shifts AI usage from isolated interactions to structured multi-agent systems, making it more suitable for real workflows and decision processes.

## Personal Insight
This approach is particularly relevant for real-world applications, where tasks are rarely isolated and require coordination between multiple steps and roles.

It suggests that the future of AI is not just better models, but better systems built around them.
